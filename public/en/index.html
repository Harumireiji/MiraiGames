What is Trackers Wonderland?

Trackers Wonderland is a serious game qui permet de se rendre compte des dangers sur Internet.


It is composed of three levels, each with a precise danger : desinformation, viruses through pop-ups, and password breaks.

Trackers Wonderland: why?

Trackers Wonderland is a serious game made by four students from the Gustave Eiffel University as part of a mentored project.

After an observation of the very fast computerization of newer generations, and the digitization of personnal data, we think it is important to raise awareness amongst players about the real dangers of Internet..

More particularly, we inform you about what is happening in the shadows and all the possible transactions that are possible with the information you input in websites.
