Welcome to the world of Trackers Wonderland...

Trackers Wonderland aims to warn teenagers of the dangers online in view of the growing threat of hackers.


In fact, more and more stolen information are being sold by said hackers. We can deal with hacking, or even an identity theft.


Thus, Trackers Wonderland is the serious game that aims to protect your personnal data.


Mirai Games-- who?

It’s a game created by a team, Mirai Games, composed of four Multimedia students, as part of a mentored project. Being a group of four young adults, we have grown with technology.


For us, the best way to raise awareness amongst younger generations is through a serious game.


Each passing day, over 1,5 million people get hacked in the world, which is over half the population of Paris. Each second, 65 data thefts occur.


In a world where data digitization develops itself day by day, the risk, too, develops itself with it.


We are trying to prevent people from an unfavorable or dangerous situation on the internet. We hope to decrease the risks with this game.
